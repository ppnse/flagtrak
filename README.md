# FlagTrak

FlagTrak is a system for managing activity for the club CTF team KattangalSec

## Goals

- Aggregate data on upcoming CTFs and activities
- Track member participation (time and metadata) across CTFs and domains
- Archive CTF challenges for practice and later review
- Expedite, deduplicate and coordinate CTF flag submissions and challenge attempts
- Assist in the review and writeup process after a CTF
- Provide a public facing leaderboard and user dashboard
- Track activities of other known teams during and outside CTF events
